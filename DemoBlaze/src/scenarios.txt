1 - Logging into the Website

 - Navigate to the DemoBlaze website.
 - Click on the "Log in" button.
 - Enter valid credentials (username and password).
 - Click the "Log in" button to access the user account.

2 - Adding a Product to the Cart

 - Navigate to the DemoBlaze home page.
 - Select a smartphone from the home page list
 - Click the "Add to cart" button.
 - Confirm the addition of the product to the cart.

3 - Contacting Customer Support

 - Navigate to the DemoBlaze home page.
 - Click on the "Contact" link.
 - Fill in the contact form with the required information.
 - Submit the form to send a message to customer support.
